---
title: ProWatermark
description: 页面水印组件，基于 Canvas 渲染重复水印覆盖层。支持多行文字、旋转角度、Tailwind 颜色类、暗色模式自适应。
category: element
---

## Usage

### Text

::component-code
---
props:
  text: Confidential
slots:
  default: |
    <div class="p-12 text-center text-muted">Protected document content here...</div>
---
::

### Rotate

::component-code
---
props:
  text: Watermark
  rotate: -20
slots:
  default: |
    <div class="p-12 text-center text-muted">Content with rotated watermark</div>
---
::

### Opacity

::component-code
---
props:
  text: Watermark
  opacity: 0.3
slots:
  default: |
    <div class="p-12 text-center text-muted">Content with low opacity watermark</div>
---
::

### Disabled

::component-code
---
props:
  text: Watermark
  disabled: true
slots:
  default: |
    <div class="p-12 text-center text-muted">Content without watermark (disabled)</div>
items:
  disabled:
    - true
    - false
---
::
